Decided (2026-07-18)
  • The unit of a like is the song. Key = folded(artist) + folded(title); album excluded, so the linked play and the ALL-CAPS free-text replay of “Call Your Name” are one liked song. artistId is an attribute, not identity.
  • Snapshot at like time. Artist, title, album, label, artwork, links, artistId — the Liked row and its detail card render from the snapshot after the play scrolls out of the feed.
  • Heal on observation. A nil-id liked song gains its artist id from any id-bearing play whose folded artist name matches (watch the log after liking “Midnight Sun”).
  • For You projection. distinct artistId across liked songs — plus liked-songs-per-artist as a free affinity weight.
  • Surfaces. Playcut row + detail card only; concert hearts deferred. Browsing: the Liked tab. Persistence: Codable file store. Analytics: counts only.
Open — the trailing slot
  • A · Stacked: heart above the info-circle. Both affordances live; the 2.5:1 row has the height. Risk: two icons compete on a clean panel.
  • B · Replace: heart takes the slot. Cleanest, most immediate; loses the explicit “tap for more” signal — the row tap alone carries it.
  • C · Detail-only: row untouched; like from the detail card; a small filled heart marks liked rows. Zero clutter, but liking what's playing right now takes two taps.
  • D · Swipe: swipe-left to like; chrome untouched; passive badge. iOS-native, but invisible until discovered.
